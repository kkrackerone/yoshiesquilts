Yoshie takes great pride in being able to display her masterpieces in local exhibits. It allows her to share the story of each quilt, and gives others the opportunity to see them.

Mother & Daughter's Colors on the Canvas 2021

When my daughter wanted to start her own business to sell quilt kits, she asked me to make quilts for her kits.This is the first quilt we made together. Unfortunately, her business didn't go well and she quit selling quilt kits. My daughter and I had such a fun time together creating quilts to sell. This quilt reminds us that mothers and daughters can create beautiful things when we are together.

Springville Museum Annual Quilt Show 2024

Garden of Quilts 2024

After I saw a pattern for this quilt on a website, I wanted to make it. I loved how the colors combined so well together. I decided to use two main colors and rearrange the pattern the way I wanted to. Weaving the colors reminds me of how we can make harmony in our lives. We are all different, but we can make this world beautiful when we get together as we weave our own colors together. My meassage with this quilt is that each of us have important parts in this beautiful world. Let's keep shining our beautiful colors!
