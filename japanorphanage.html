Story of Japanese Orphanage

Some years ago I watched TV show about two years old girl getting adopted from orphanage in Japan to American couple. Kids who live in the orphanage get at least one person to take care of as a caregiver. This girl was so attached to the caregiver. The day when she departed to America with the American couple, she was so sad to say bye to her caregiver since this caregiver was the only one who took care of her just like her own mom. This happens all kids or babies who live at the orphanage. They have to be separated by those who take care of them for a year or two years since they cannot stay there more than two years. I felt so sorry for those kids who have to have the experience at the early age. I wanted those kids to know that someone care for them always by giving a quilt. I contacted with the orphanage and see if I can make a quilt for kids. They so welcomed to receive my quilt. I have been making a quilt for kids or babies who are adapted and leave the orphanage for almost two years. I get pictures and letters from couples who receive my quilt with their adapted kids or babies.
